Facts

Showcasing Films

"Watch it Outside" provide a platform for filmmakers to showcase their work to a diverse audience, including industry professionals, critics, and the general public. This exposure can lead to recognition, distribution deals, and opportunities for emerging filmmakers.

Networking and Collaboration

"Watch it Outside" bring together filmmakers, producers, actors, distributors, and other industry professionals, fostering networking opportunities. These events often serve as hubs for collaboration, where people can discuss ideas, form partnerships, and create future projects.

Cultural Exchange

"Watch it Outside" frequently feature movies from various cultures, shedding light on different perspectives, traditions, and stories. This cultural exchange helps in broadening audience horizons and promoting diversity and understanding.

Promotion and Marketing

Our festivals serve as a marketing platform, generating buzz and publicity for films. Positive reviews, awards, and recognition at festivals can attract media attention, build anticipation, and increase a film's commercial success.

Community Engagement

Our festivals often engage local communities, drawing people together to celebrate cinema. This can enhance cultural appreciation, boost tourism, and create a sense of unity within the hosting city or region.

Experiencing Unconventional Films

"Watch it Outside" often screen films that might not get mainstream attention but are artistically bold, experimental, or deal with niche subjects. This allows audiences to explore unconventional and thought-provoking cinema.

Education and Discussion

"Watch it Outside" organize panels, workshops, and Q&A sessions with filmmakers, providing educational opportunities for both aspiring and established professionals. These discussions delve into the creative process, industry trends, and the challenges of filmmaking.

About

Welcome to "Watch it Outside" your gateway to immersive outdoor movie experiences! We believe in the magic of cinema under
the open sky, bringing communities together for unforgettable movie nights.
At "Watch it Outside," we are passionate about creating moments that transcend the traditional movie-watching experience.
Our mission is to transform public spaces into captivating outdoor theaters, providing a platform for people to connect, relax,
and enjoy the enchantment of films amidst nature's embrace.
What sets us apart is our dedication to curating a diverse selection of movies that cater to varied tastes and age groups.
Whether you're a fan of classic blockbusters, indie gems, family favorites, or cinematic masterpieces, we've got something special for everyone.
We're thrilled to have received approval to host free movie screenings in the beautiful setting of Boston Public Garden.
From August 5th through 8th, join us for an extraordinary cinematic journey from 6:00 PM to midnight.
To ensure a cozy spot, be sure to pre-register as seating is limited to 1,500 seats per screening.
"Watch it Outside" isn't just about movies; it's about community, shared experiences, and creating cherished memories. We invite you
to be a part of this cinematic adventure and rediscover the joy of watching movies under the stars.
Join us, bring your friends and family, and let's make these evenings in the Boston Public Garden an experience to remember.
Thank you for being a part of our story. Let's make every screening a night to remember!
